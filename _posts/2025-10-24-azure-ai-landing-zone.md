If you are looking to deploy AI apps or agents in a production environment, the Azure AI Landing Zone is worth checking out. It provides an enterprise-scale reference architecture with implementations in Portal, Bicep, and Terraform to deploy secure and resilient AI solutions in Azure.

I like that it covers the full stack from infrastructure to security, so you don't have to figure out each piece separately.
